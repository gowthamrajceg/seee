On the expedition of hunting for its writers , the Impulse team never forgets to inspire a whole lot of young novice writers to come forward and write articles. However, the number of articles the team had been receiving was so numerous that the team was unfortunately not able to print all of them in a codified magazine. In order to make up for it, the team decided to publish those articles in its online blog. Unexpectedly this has widened the readers' circle as now these are available for the whole world to read. A number of students and distinguished professors across the globe give a read to these articles which further inspire the writers to write more and more.
